# SOC 2 PBC Tracker (Prepared By Client)

This tracker helps you manage and submit audit artifacts required during a SOC 2 Type I or Type II engagement. It outlines documentation typically requested by your auditor and tracks status, owners, and delivery dates.

---

## Tracker Format

| # | Artifact Description                                | Owner           | System/Tool        | Due Date   | Delivered? | Notes |
|---|-----------------------------------------------------|------------------|--------------------|------------|------------|-------|
| 1 | GRC Program Charter                                 | GRC Manager      | Confluence         | 2025-10-01 | ✅         | Uploaded to SharePoint |
| 2 | Access Control Policy                               | Security Officer | Policy Portal      | 2025-10-01 | ✅         | Include version history |
| 3 | User Access Review Evidence (Q3)                    | IAM Lead         | Okta / Jira        | 2025-10-03 | ✅         | Use export from Q3 UAR |
| 4 | Risk Assessment + Risk Register                     | Risk Analyst     | Excel              | 2025-10-04 | ✅         | Dated within 6 months |
| 5 | Change Management SOP + Sample CRs                  | Governance Lead  | Jira Service Mgmt  | 2025-10-05 | ✅         | Include screenshots |
| 6 | Incident Management Logs + IR Policy                | Incident Lead    | Jira / PDF         | 2025-10-06 | ✅         | Redact sensitive info |
| 7 | Security Awareness Training Records                 | HR               | LMS (TalentLMS)    | 2025-10-07 | ✅         | Certificates exported |
| 8 | Vendor Risk Assessments (Top 10 Vendors)            | Procurement      | Torri / Google Doc | 2025-10-08 | ✅         | Attach signed DPAs |
| 9 | Audit Logs for Key Systems (90 Days)                | IT Ops           | AWS, Azure, Jira   | 2025-10-09 | ✅         | Export as CSV or PDF |
| 10 | Evidence of Control Monitoring / Testing           | Governance Lead  | AuditBoard / Jira  | 2025-10-10 | ✅         | Include test dates |
| 11 | Board/Exec Risk Review Meeting Notes (Last 12 mo)  | CIO              | Confluence         | 2025-10-11 | ✅         | Use meeting export |
| 12 | Termination Access Revocation Logs                 | IT / HR          | Jira / Okta        | 2025-10-12 | ✅         | Track timing post-exit |

---

## Usage Tips

- Track in parallel with your auditor’s PBC request list.
- Include delivery links to your shared drive or audit portal.
- Update status frequently and assign clear owners.
- Maintain version control of documents submitted.
