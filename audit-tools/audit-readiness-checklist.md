# Audit Readiness Checklist

Use this checklist to prepare your organization for internal or external IT audits (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, etc.). This template is intended to help governance teams gather documentation, validate control effectiveness, and ensure audit success.

---

## Documentation Preparedness

- [ ] ISMS or GRC program charter is documented and up to date
- [ ] Acceptable Use, Access Control, and Data Protection policies are finalized and version-controlled
- [ ] Control owners are assigned and trained
- [ ] Asset inventory is documented (hardware, software, cloud)
- [ ] Risk register is complete and reviewed within the last 6 months

---

## Access & Security Controls

- [ ] User access reviews are conducted and logged (quarterly or monthly)
- [ ] Role-based access control (RBAC) is enforced
- [ ] Privileged accounts are monitored and reviewed
- [ ] MFA is enabled for all critical systems
- [ ] Termination/offboarding processes include access removal

---

## Evidence Collection

- [ ] Audit logs are collected, reviewed, and stored securely
- [ ] Screenshots, exports, or system reports are gathered as audit evidence
- [ ] All control test results are documented and dated
- [ ] Change management and incident logs are ready for review
- [ ] Vendor due diligence documents are current (SOC 2, DPA, SLA, etc.)

---

## Control Testing Readiness

- [ ] Testing schedule is in place for controls (manual or automated)
- [ ] Controls are mapped to applicable frameworks (e.g., ISO 27001 Annex A, NIST 800-53)
- [ ] Prior year audit findings (if any) are tracked with remediation evidence

---

## Tools & Systems

- [ ] GRC platform or tracker is in use (Jira, AuditBoard, spreadsheet, etc.)
- [ ] All relevant policies, trackers, and templates are accessible to auditors
- [ ] Backup, recovery, and incident response documentation is available
- [ ] Metrics and dashboards (e.g., SLA performance, incident trends) are available on request

---

## Notes

Use this checklist throughout the audit planning and fieldwork phases. Mark completion dates and ownership for each item. Keep a versioned copy for each audit cycle.
