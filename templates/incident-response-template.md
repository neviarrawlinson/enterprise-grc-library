# Incident Response Template

Use this template to document operational or security incidents, investigation details, containment efforts, and postmortem findings.

---

## Incident Summary

- **Incident ID**: `INC-YYYY-XXX`
- **Title**: Brief one-line summary of the incident
- **Date/Time Detected**: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM
- **Date/Time Resolved**: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM
- **Reported By**: Name or team
- **Detected By**: Monitoring tool, end-user, team, etc.
- **Impact Area**: Systems, teams, customers affected
- **Severity**: Critical / High / Medium / Low
- **Status**: Open / In Progress / Resolved / Closed

---

## Description of the Issue

Describe what happened in plain language. Include timeline of events if available.

---

## Investigation & Root Cause

- What caused the issue?
- What tools, logs, or methods were used to investigate?
- Who was involved in the investigation?

---

## Containment, Mitigation, & Recovery

- Steps taken to stop the issue
- Actions taken to restore service
- Any temporary fixes or workarounds applied

---

## Customer / Business Impact

- How many users were affected?
- What services were unavailable?
- Were SLAs breached?
- Was there any data loss or breach?

---

## Resolution & Corrective Actions

- Final fix or remediation steps taken
- Infrastructure or code changes made
- Runbook or policy updates completed

---

## Governance Notes

- Was the incident linked to a change?
- Was an RCA conducted? (link if applicable)
- Were internal policies followed?
- Evidence retained for audit?

---

## Lessons Learned

- What went well?
- What could have been done better?
- How will we prevent this in the future?
