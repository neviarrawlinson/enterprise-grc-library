# GRC Learning Resources

This GRC Learning Guide is a curated resource collection for those entering or growing within Governance, Risk, and Compliance. Whether you are transitioning into the field or strengthening your current expertise, the resources below will help you build a strong foundation in GRC.

## Books

Explore foundational and modern GRC concepts through these recommended titles:

- *Measuring and Managing Information Risk* by Jack Freund and Jack Jones (FAIR Framework)
- *The Phoenix Project* by Gene Kim
- *Cybersecurity and Cyberwar* by P.W. Singer and Allan Friedman
- *IT Governance* by Peter Weill and Jeanne Ross
- *How to Measure Anything in Cybersecurity Risk* by Douglas Hubbard and Richard Seiersen

## Certifications

Consider these certifications to build credibility and advance in your GRC career:

| Certification | Issuing Organization | Focus Area |
|---------------|-----------------------|------------|
| CRISC | ISACA | Risk and Control Management |
| ISO 27001 Lead Implementer | PECB, BSI, or TUV | Information Security Management Systems |
| GRC Analyst Masterclass | Breaking Into Cybersecurity | Entry-Level GRC Concepts |
| CGRCP | OCEG | Governance Frameworks |
| CompTIA Security+ | CompTIA | General Cybersecurity |
| NIST RMF Training | NIST | Federal Risk Management Framework |
