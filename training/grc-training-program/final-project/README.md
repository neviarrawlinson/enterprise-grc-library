## Your Deliverables

Create the following as part of your final project:

1. **Risk Register**  
   - At least 5 risks with scoring, owners, and mitigation plans

2. **Control Mapping Table**  
   - Map ISO 27001 Annex A controls to either NIST CSF or your own internal controls

3. **Policy Document**  
   - Create an Acceptable Use Policy or Data Retention Policy

4. **Change Management Checklist**  
   - Provide a ready-to-use checklist with categories and explanations

5. **Audit PBC Tracker**  
   - Include 5 sample controls with test steps and evidence references

6. **GRC Dashboard Mockup**  
   - Create a one-page report summarizing risks, open audit items, and compliance KPIs
