# Module 8: GRC Tools, Platforms, and Automation

## Overview

This module introduces key tools and platforms used in Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) programs, including purpose-built GRC software, cloud-based compliance platforms, and automation solutions. Learners will gain familiarity with the landscape of tools that support audits, risk tracking, policy management, vendor due diligence, change management, and more.

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

- Identify and categorize common GRC tools and platforms by function (audit, risk, compliance, etc.)
- Compare features of leading GRC software vendors
- Understand the role of automation in modern compliance programs
- Evaluate tool fit based on organizational size, maturity, and regulatory needs
- Review open source and low-cost tools suitable for small organizations

---

## Topics Covered

- Categories of GRC Tools:
  - Audit & Compliance Management
  - Risk Assessment & Risk Register Platforms
  - Policy & SOP Management Tools
  - Vendor & Third-Party Risk Tools
  - Workflow and Change Management (e.g., Jira)
  - Training and Awareness Platforms
  - Cloud Compliance Platforms (e.g., Drata, Vanta, Anecdotes)
- Intro to GRC Automation:
  - What can be automated?
  - Example: Risk scoring logic
  - Example: Policy attestation workflows
- Evaluating Tools:
  - Key questions to ask vendors
  - Demo prep checklist
  - SaaS security considerations

---

## Activities

1. Research and document at least 5 GRC platforms.
2. Compare Drata vs Anecdotes vs Vanta: create a side-by-side feature table.
3. Download and explore an open-source GRC tool (e.g., GRC Toolbox, OpenGRC).
4. Analyze a Jira-based change or risk tracker—how could it be improved?
