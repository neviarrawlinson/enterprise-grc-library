# Module 01: Introduction to Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)

Welcome to Module 1 of the GRC Training Program. This foundational module will introduce you to the core concepts, scope, and purpose of Governance, Risk, and Compliance. By the end of this module, you will understand the role of GRC professionals and how GRC aligns with business strategy, IT, cybersecurity, and regulatory requirements.

---

## Learning Objectives

After completing this module, you will be able to:

- Define Governance, Risk, and Compliance and explain how they interrelate.
- Describe the importance of GRC to modern organizations.
- Identify the key domains within GRC.
- Differentiate between GRC roles and career paths.
- Recognize real-world use cases of GRC in various industries.

---

## Topics Covered

- What is GRC?
- The GRC Triad: Governance, Risk, Compliance
- Why GRC Matters in Modern Organizations
- Types of GRC Frameworks (overview)
- GRC in Action: Sample Use Cases
- Careers in GRC: Analyst, Auditor, Manager, Consultant
- GRC Stakeholders: IT, Security, Legal, HR, and more
