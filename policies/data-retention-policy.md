## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the minimum and maximum retention periods for organizational data, ensuring legal, regulatory, and business requirements are met while minimizing risk and storage costs.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- Electronic records and files
- Emails and chat messages
- System and access logs
- Cloud storage and SaaS platforms
- Physical documents (where applicable)
