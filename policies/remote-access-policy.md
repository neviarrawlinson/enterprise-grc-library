# Remote Access Policy Template

## Purpose

This policy defines the requirements and responsibilities for accessing ClearCaptions’ internal systems remotely to ensure secure connectivity and prevent unauthorized access to sensitive data.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- All employees, contractors, and third-party users who access ClearCaptions systems remotely
- All devices (company-owned or BYOD) used to access internal systems from remote locations

---

## Policy Requirements

| Control Area              | Requirement                                                                 |
|---------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Authentication**        | Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all remote connections   |
| **VPN Use**               | Remote access must occur over an approved, encrypted VPN                    |
| **Device Standards**      | Devices must have updated antivirus, firewalls, and OS patches               |
| **Monitoring**            | All remote sessions must be logged and monitored                            |
| **Access Scope**          | Only necessary systems should be accessible remotely (least privilege)       |
| **Idle Timeout**          | Sessions must auto-disconnect after 15 minutes of inactivity                 |
| **Prohibited Activities** | Remote access from public/shared computers is not allowed                   |

---

## Responsibilities

- Users must report lost or compromised devices immediately
- IT must maintain logs of remote access sessions for at least 90 days
- Users must not disable endpoint security controls while working remotely

---

## Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, including suspension of access, reporting to HR, or contract termination. Security breaches traced to poor remote access hygiene may be escalated to the incident response team.

---

## Review Cycle

This policy will be reviewed annually or upon changes in remote work strategy, infrastructure, or compliance obligations.
