# Access Control Policy Template

## Purpose

To ensure that access to ClearCaptions’ systems and data is granted based on the principle of least privilege and is managed securely throughout the user lifecycle.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- All employees, contractors, and third-party users
- All systems, applications, databases, and network resources
- All access methods, including on-premises and remote

---

## Policy Statements

### 1. User Access Management

- All users must have unique IDs and accounts
- Access must be requested via formal process (e.g., Jira ticket)
- Manager and system owner approvals are required before granting access
- Access must align with defined job roles and business need

### 2. Least Privilege Principle

- Users will be given the minimum level of access required for their role
- Administrative access must be tightly controlled and reviewed quarterly

### 3. Authentication Requirements

- All users must authenticate using strong passwords and multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Service accounts must be non-interactive and rotated regularly

### 4. Access Reviews

- Quarterly access reviews must be conducted for all high-risk systems
- Reviews will be documented and retained for audit purposes

### 5. Termination and Revocation

- Access must be revoked within 24 hours of user termination or role change
- IT must coordinate with HR and system owners for deprovisioning

---

## Roles and Responsibilities

| Role                | Responsibility                                                  |
|---------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|
| IT Governance       | Defines access policies and coordinates reviews                 |
| System Owners       | Approves access to owned systems and participates in reviews    |
| HR                  | Notifies IT of terminations or role changes                     |
| Managers            | Initiate access requests and confirm access validity            |
| End Users           | Use access responsibly and report anomalies                     |

---

## Enforcement

Unauthorized access or misuse may result in disciplinary action, access revocation, or legal consequences.

---

## Review Cycle

This policy is reviewed annually or upon significant system or organizational changes.
