# Risk Assessment Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure all key steps are completed during your risk assessment process.

---

## Planning Phase
- [ ] Define assessment scope and objectives
- [ ] Identify stakeholders and approvers
- [ ] Select risk methodology (qualitative / quantitative)
- [ ] Schedule risk workshops or interviews

---

## Risk Identification
- [ ] List all assets (data, apps, systems, processes)
- [ ] Identify potential threats
- [ ] Identify related vulnerabilities
- [ ] Document risk scenarios and causes

---

## Risk Analysis
- [ ] Evaluate likelihood of each risk
- [ ] Evaluate impact of each risk
- [ ] Assign risk scores (heatmap, matrix, scale)
- [ ] Categorize risks by severity

---

## Risk Treatment Planning
- [ ] Define mitigation strategies
- [ ] Assign risk owners
- [ ] Set deadlines for treatment
- [ ] Determine residual risk level

---

## Risk Documentation
- [ ] Enter all risks in Risk Register
- [ ] Ensure traceability from source to mitigation
- [ ] Include reviewer comments / notes

---

## eview & Sign-off
- [ ] Conduct stakeholder review
- [ ] Finalize risk report or presentation
- [ ] Obtain formal sign-off
