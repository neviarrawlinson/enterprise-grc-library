# SOC 2 Readiness Checklist

Use this checklist to validate your organization's readiness for a SOC 2 Type I or Type II audit.

---

## General Preparation

- [ ] Assign a SOC 2 project lead
- [ ] Identify audit scope (Type I or Type II, systems, locations, controls)
- [ ] Select Trust Services Criteria (Security is mandatory; Availability, Confidentiality, Privacy, and Processing Integrity are optional)
- [ ] Engage an auditor or SOC 2 readiness consultant
- [ ] Choose a GRC platform or tool for evidence tracking

---

## Security (Common Criteria)

- [ ] Formal Information Security Policy approved by leadership
- [ ] Acceptable Use Policy communicated to staff
- [ ] Endpoint protection and patch management in place
- [ ] Multi-factor authentication (MFA) enforced on critical systems
- [ ] Role-based access controls defined and enforced
- [ ] Logical and physical access reviews performed regularly
- [ ] Vendor risk assessments completed and documented
- [ ] Risk register maintained and reviewed
- [ ] Security awareness training conducted at least annually

---

## Availability

- [ ] System uptime SLAs defined and monitored
- [ ] Backup and disaster recovery procedures tested
- [ ] Monitoring tools implemented to detect outages or anomalies

---

## Confidentiality

- [ ] Data classification policy exists
- [ ] Encryption in transit and at rest is enforced
- [ ] Data retention and disposal policies defined and followed

---

## Privacy (if applicable)

- [ ] Privacy policy published and accessible
- [ ] Consent and data subject rights management documented
- [ ] Data processing agreements in place with subprocessors

---

## Audit Evidence & Controls

- [ ] Controls documented and mapped to TSC requirements
- [ ] Evidence collected for each control (logs, screenshots, configs, reports)
- [ ] Controls tested over time (for Type II audits)
- [ ] Internal audit or pre-assessment completed

---

## Final Steps Before Audit

- [ ] SOC 2 project kickoff completed
- [ ] Control owners identified and briefed
- [ ] Evidence stored in centralized system (GRC tool, Confluence, shared drive)
- [ ] Key personnel available during audit period
- [ ] Audit timeline and communication plan agreed upon
